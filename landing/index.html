Features

Everything you need to
turn speech into text.

From instant dictation to full subtitle generation, Annotate is built for people who think faster than they type.

Push-to-Talk

Hold your custom hotkey, speak naturally, release — transcribed text is instantly pasted at your cursor position.

Fully Offline

Run Whisper AI locally with CUDA acceleration. Your audio never leaves your machine — complete privacy by design.

Subtitle Generation

Generate precise SRT subtitles from any audio or video file using Voice Activity Detection and Whisper.

Custom Dictionary

Add specialized terms, names, and jargon to boost transcription accuracy for your specific domain.

Grammar Cleanup

Automatically fix grammar and punctuation in transcriptions using a local AI model — no cloud needed.

Cloud Sync

Sign in with Google to sync your dictionary and transcription history seamlessly across all your devices.

How It Works

Three steps. Zero friction.

From installation to your first transcription in under a minute.

Set your hotkey

Choose any keyboard shortcut. Ctrl+Shift+Space, a function key — whatever feels natural to you.

Hold & speak

Press and hold your hotkey anywhere on your system. A subtle indicator confirms it's listening.

Release & paste

Let go and the transcribed text is instantly typed wherever your cursor is — any app, any field.

Cloud

Groq Whisper

Lightning-fast cloud transcription via Groq's API. Best accuracy with minimal latency - just add an API key.

Minimum

  • CPU: 2 cores / 4 threads
  • RAM: 4 GB
  • GPU: Not required
  • Storage: 1 GB free
  • Internet: Stable upload + low packet loss

Recommended

  • CPU: 4+ cores
  • RAM: 8 GB
  • GPU: Not required
  • Storage: 2 GB free SSD
  • Internet: Low-latency route to API region
Offline

Local Whisper

Run Whisper large-v3-turbo locally with Vulkan-capable acceleration. Complete privacy, no internet required.

Minimum

  • CPU: 4 cores / 8 threads
  • RAM: 8 GB
  • GPU: Vulkan 1.2 support, 4 GB VRAM
  • Storage: 4 GB free SSD
  • OS: Windows 10 22H2 or newer

Recommended

  • CPU: 8+ cores / 16 threads
  • RAM: 16 GB (32 GB for long subtitle jobs)
  • GPU: Vulkan-capable discrete GPU, 8 GB VRAM
  • Storage: 20 GB free NVMe SSD
  • OS: Windows 11 (latest stable)
